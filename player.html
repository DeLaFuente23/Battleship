Private Battleship Screen

Your fleet

Tap one ship on the right, then tap the board to place it. Use the rotate button before placing. After both players are ready, keep using this board to mark the coordinates your opponent calls out.

Place your ships
Select a ship, place all 5 on the board, then lock them with the ready button.

Fleet

All ships must fit inside the 10x10 board and cannot overlap.

How the marks work

On your own board after setup: tapping water marks a miss, tapping one of your ships marks a hit, and sunk ships fade automatically.

On the enemy board: taps cycle through attack marker, miss, hit, then clear, so you can track the battle manually.

Attack Board

Pick a coordinate to attack, tell it to your opponent, then update the marker based on what they tell you.
