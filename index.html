2 Player Game

Battleship

Enter exactly two player names, generate one private link for each person, and use those pages to place ships and play.

Only two names are needed. Each player gets their own private page and QR code.

1. Share the pages

Each player opens only their own link or scans only their own QR code.

2. Place ships

On their private page, each player sets all 5 ships on a 10x10 board and locks them in.

3. Call coordinates

The attack grid sits below the main board, so players can track turns and incoming shots manually.

Player Links

Each player gets one direct link and one QR code to their own Battleship screen.
